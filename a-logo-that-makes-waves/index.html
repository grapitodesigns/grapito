A Logo That Makes Waves..!

Designed a bold and impactful image-based logo for Samudra Fishland, a seafood sales and export business.

Samudra Fishland, a seafood sales and export company, needed a strong image-based logo that instantly conveyed their industry and expertise. The challenge was to create a design that reflected freshness, trust, and the essence of the ocean. Our approach incorporated marine-inspired elements, ensuring the logo was visually striking, memorable, and resonated with their brand identity.
