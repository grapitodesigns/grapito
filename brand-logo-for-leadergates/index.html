Brand Logo for Leadergates

Designed a logo for LeaderGate, an automatic door solutions provider in UAE, ensuring it reflects their products and services at first glance.

LeaderGate, a provider of automatic door sales and services in the UAE, required a logo that instantly communicated their expertise. The challenge was to create a design that visually represented both automation and security while maintaining a modern, professional look. Our approach combined clean, dynamic elements that symbolized movement and reliability, ensuring the brand identity aligned perfectly with their industry and services
